⚒️ Easy Fix

It's quite easy; all you have to do is copy the script from BookmarkCopy.js after which you may add the script to your bookmarks bar. Depending on the website you are on, a popup will open on click. After clicking the bookmark you added click export to save the file from the game on the old site, then navigate to the new X-Code Gam3s website URL and on the same game you were on then click the bookmark, then hit import to load the previously downloaded file, and you're done!
